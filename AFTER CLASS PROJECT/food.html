Indian Cuisines

The traditional food of India has been widely appreciated for its fabulous use of herbs and spices. Indian cuisine is known for its large assortment of dishes. The cooking style varies from region to region and is divided into South Indian and North Indian cuisine. India is famous for its diverse multicultural food.

  • Bengali
  • Gujarati
  • Mughlai
  • Punjabi
  • South Indian

Bengali Food

Bengali cuisine is appreciated for its fabulous use of panchphoron, a term used to refer to the five essential spices: mustard, fenugreek seed, cumin seed, aniseed, and black cumin seed.

Gujarati Food

Traditional Gujarati food is mainly vegetarian and has high nutritional value. Gujarati thali includes many delicious dishes and flavors.

Mughlai Food

Mughlai cuisine is one of the most popular cuisines, with origins that can be traced back to the Mughal Empire.

Punjabi Food

Punjabi cuisine has a huge variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. It is known for its rich flavors and spices.

South Indian Cuisine

South Indian cuisine is known for flavorful rice-based dishes such as dosas, idlis, vadas, and uttapams.
