STRANGER THINGS

The complete fan encyclopedia: Characters, Actors, Seasons, and the Upside Down

Overview

Stranger Things is a sci-fi horror series created by the Duffer Brothers, premiering on Netflix in 2016. Set in Hawkins, Indiana in the 1980s, the story follows a group of kids investigating mysterious events, including the disappearance of Will Byers and the appearance of Eleven, a girl with psychic abilities. The series explores friendship, trauma, government conspiracies, and supernatural dimensions, blending 80s nostalgia with horror, suspense, and adventure.

Characters

Eleven / Jane Hopper

Millie Bobby Brown. Psychic girl escaping Hawkins Lab; central to fighting the Upside Down.

Mike Wheeler

Finn Wolfhard. Leader of the friend group; close bond with Eleven.

Lucas Sinclair

Caleb McLaughlin. Tactical, brave, protective.

Dustin Henderson

Gaten Matarazzo. Science enthusiast and comic relief.

Will Byers

Noah Schnapp. Disappeared in S1, often possessed by the Mind Flayer.

Max Mayfield

Sadie Sink. Brave new friend, skateboarder, struggles with family and trauma.

Nancy Wheeler

Natalia Dyer. Mike’s older sister, strong-willed and investigative.

Jonathan Byers

Charlie Heaton. Will’s protective older brother.

Steve Harrington

Joe Keery. Popular teen turned protector; mentor to Dustin & friends.

Robin Buckley

Maya Hawke. Intelligent ally introduced in S3; helps decode Russian secrets.

Erica Sinclair

Priah Ferguson. Lucas’s assertive little sister; helps in battles against the Upside Down.

Jim Hopper

David Harbour. Hawkins police chief, guardian figure for Eleven.

Joyce Byers

Winona Ryder. Mother of Will and Jonathan; determined, fearless.

Martin Brenner

Matthew Modine. Head of Hawkins Lab; experiments with children, including Eleven.

Murray Bauman

Brett Gelman. Conspiracy theorist; helps uncover government secrets.

Sam Owens

Paul Reiser. Government scientist; works with Hopper & the kids to contain threats.

Vecna / Henry Creel

Jamie Campbell Bower. Main villain from S4–S5; former Hawkins Lab test subject turned deadly force.

Billy Hargrove

Dacre Montgomery. Max’s volatile stepbrother; becomes Mind Flayer host.

Victor Creel

Robert Englund. Introduced in S4; terrifying backstory linked to Upside Down.

Karen Wheeler

Cara Buono. Mother of Mike and Nancy.

Bob Newby

Sean Astin. Joyce’s boyfriend in S2.

Argyle

Eduardo Franco. Jonathan’s friend in S4.

Alexei

Alec Utgoff. Russian scientist, lovable ally in S3.

Claudia Henderson

Catherine Curtin. Dustin’s mom.

Mr. Clarke

Randy Havens. Hawkins school teacher who helps the kids.

Heather Holloway

Francesca Reale. Victim in S4 subplot.

Lieutenant Detective Hanson

John Reynolds. Police ally in S1.

Father Owens

Will Chase. Church figure tied to government secrets.

Chrissy Cunningham

Grace Van Dien. Cheerleader with tragic arc in S4.

Seasons

Season 1 (2016)

Will Byers disappears; Eleven appears; first breach into the Upside Down.

Season 2 (2017)

The Mind Flayer emerges; Will is possessed; the group tries to close the portal.

Season 3 (2019)

Russian secret base opens; Mind Flayer manifests physically; Starcourt Mall battle.

Season 4 (2022)

Vecna is revealed; multiple breaches to the Upside Down; Hawkins faces its darkest hour.

Season 5 (Upcoming)

The final season, expected to wrap up the Hawkins story and the Upside Down.

The Upside Down

A parallel dark dimension mirroring Hawkins, inhabited by deadly creatures like the Demogorgon and Vecna. Features ash-like spores, twisted vines, and an eerie atmosphere. Accessed through interdimensional breaches, usually linked to Hawkins Lab experiments.
